Features

Everything your prep demands

Built for athletes who take their protocols as seriously as their training.

Bloodwork Tracking
Log every panel and trend key markers over time. Sparklines, grade ratings, and reference ranges so you always know where you stand.
Protocol Management
Track compounds, doses, and frequencies with pinpoint accuracy. Compliance streaks keep you accountable every single day.
Weekly Check-Ins
Mood, sleep quality, libido, pumps, energy, and body condition — all captured in one fast check-in tap. Built for busy athletes.
Hevy Integration
Auto-sync workouts directly from Hevy. No manual logging. Your training volume, PRs, and exercise history live right alongside your protocol data.
Coach Layer
Self-coach or connect a real coach to your dashboard. Share your data via secure token. Coaches get their own athlete roster with full protocol visibility.
Daily Readiness Score
HRV + sleep + compliance + wellness averaged into one actionable number every morning. Know when to push hard and when to recover smart.
