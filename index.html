Why it matters

What goes wrong without tracking

Most people on HRT rely on memory and gut feeling. Data reveals patterns that intuition misses every time.

1
Changing multiple variables at once — no way to know what actually worked
2
Switching protocols before they reach steady state (4–6 weeks minimum)
3
Chasing lab numbers instead of symptom resolution and how you actually feel
4
Not correlating bloodwork timing to injection schedule — trough vs. peak matters
5
No record to bring to your provider — appointments based on memory alone
6
Forgetting which compounds you ran, at what dose, and what the outcome was
